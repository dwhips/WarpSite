Hiking
Donut Falls
Check out this easier 3.3-mile out-and-back trail a 40 minute drive from downtown Salt Lake City. The colors are beautiful this time of year, and those feeling adventurous can climb a scramble to a unique water fall.

View Trail
Little Mountain Summit
This hike begins with a 20 minute drive through the east mountains and over a reservoir. Even the drive to the hike has incredible views. There are a few well maintained trails to choose, one of which take you along the mountain ridge to enjoy two more mountains on both sides. A great hike.
