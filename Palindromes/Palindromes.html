It's time to explore palindromes! Don't nod, palindromes are a word, phrase, number or sequence of language that read the same forwards and backwards. Try the next few examples

  • Mom
  • Kayak
  • Top spot
  • Never odd or even
  • Red rum, sir, is murder

As we have seen, palindromes can form words and sentences of varying difficulty. It can also be seen in interesting ways in math. For instance see what happens when we multiply numbers only containing "1".
